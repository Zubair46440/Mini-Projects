Record Breaking Placements

GLOBAL has earned fame for its exceptional placement record. We boast the best placement rate in central India, with all eligible students securing positions in various prestigious MNCs. Last year, our students were successfully placed in renowned companies like TCS, Wipro, Infosys, Cognizant, L&T Infotech, Persistent, KPIT Technologies, Zycus, Zensar, Hexaware, Mphasis, and many others.
Our commitment to providing quality education and fostering industry connections ensures that our graduates find rewarding opportunities in the corporate world. We maintain strong ties with top recruiters, offering our students unparalleled access to career-launching opportunities. Our dedicated placement cell works tirelessly to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in their chosen fields. Additionally, we conduct regular workshops, mock interviews, and training sessions to prepare our students for the competitive job market. Our alumni network, spread across various leading companies globally, also plays a crucial role in mentoring and guiding current students.
